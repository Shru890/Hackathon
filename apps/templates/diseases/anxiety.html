Description

Anxiety is a feeling of unease, such as worry or fear, that can be mild or severe. Everyone has feelings of anxiety at some point in their life. For example, you may feel worried and anxious about sitting an exam or having a job interview

Symptoms

restlessness, a sense of dread, feeling constantly "on edge", difficulty concentrating, irritability

Precautions

walking fast or jogging, swimming, any sport, distract yourself

Cures

Learn to relax, Avoid caffeine, Avoid smoking and drinking, Avoid overthinking
