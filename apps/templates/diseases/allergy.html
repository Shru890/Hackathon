ALLERGY

  • Description

    An allergy is a reaction the body has to a particular food or substance. Allergies are very common. Having an allergy can be a nuisance and affect your everyday activities, but most allergic reactions are mild and can be largely kept under control.

  • Symptoms

    sneezing, a runny or blocked nose, red/itchy/watery eyes, wheezing and coughing, a red/itchy rash, worsening of asthma or eczema symptoms.

  • Precautions

    The reasons for this are not understood, but one of the main theories is it's the result of living in a cleaner, germ-free environment, which reduces the number of germs our immune system has to deal with. It's thought this may cause it to overreact.

  • Cures

    Eat Healthy, Wear a Mask, Wash yourself.
